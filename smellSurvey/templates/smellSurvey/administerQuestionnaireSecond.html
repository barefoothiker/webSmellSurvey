What type of questions will be asked?

We will ask questions about:

  • Your smell and taste symptoms (e.g. onset date, characteristic….)
  • What may have caused these symptoms
  • Received medical intervention (e.g. surgery, drugs, if it worked..)
  • Other signs or conditions that may be associated with smell and taste issues
  • Your past medical history
  • Allergies
  • Family history
  • Social and professional environment

… and we will not ask your name.
